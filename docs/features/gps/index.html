GPS

Our GPS tracker is on point

Never get lost and stay right on track with our GPS tracker!

  • Never miss a turn

    As soon as you start the walk or hike, you will see which directions to take to get to the destination without problems. Get step-by-step voice directions so you can easily put your phone or tablet away and enjoy the view to the fullest.

  • Offline mode

    Always know where you are, even when you are not connected to the internet. Using offline navigation will also help with the battery life of your phone or tablet.

  • Find campsites

    If you feel like you need a break, or want to call it a day and get some sleep, you can find possible campsites on the map.
